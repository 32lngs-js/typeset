TypeSet

Typography is the interface.

A product's voice lives in its type. This page has nothing to sell — it's here so you can grab any word and scrub it.

Open the panel in the corner, click any piece of text, and edit its size, weight, measure, and face live. Shift-click or drag a marquee to change several at once, then Copy CSS for real selector blocks you can paste back into your stylesheet.

Nothing here is precious. Every change is a live inline style, so a reload wipes the slate. Copy is how you keep what you like.
